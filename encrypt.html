Encrypt a post

Paste your markdown (with front matter), set a password, and click Encrypt. Save the output as posts/<slug>.md.enc in your repo, then add an entry to CONTENT.blog with locked: true.

Algorithm: AES-GCM (256-bit key) with PBKDF2-SHA-256, 600,000 iterations. Random 16-byte salt and 12-byte IV per encryption. Nothing leaves your browser.

 

Save the output as a single line in posts/<slug>.md.enc. Forgetting the password = the post is unrecoverable.
