Sometimes I enjoy writing posts that most people might not be able to appreciate.

### Well, dear reader, that is precisely the reason as to why I added locked posts to the blog!

If you are a close associate that I allow to read some of them, then I will give you the access password. The posts themselves **technically** are visible on github but encrypted with SHA256, so good luck trying to reverse-engineer anything.

In these posts I will mainly just talk and ideate about books, concepts I find interesting and give my interpretations slash reviews slash modifications. Locked posts are necessary, in my opinion, because most of these books, concepts will most likely lean more left than people are used to and I don't want to alienate anyone!

Also, I added LaTeX editing to my blogs, so expect a lot of $E = mc^2 + AI$ soon. Thanks for reading!
